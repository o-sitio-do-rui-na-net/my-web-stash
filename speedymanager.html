about Speedy Manager

In this fast-paced world we often find ourselves surrounded with numerous tasks and projects that we set in the nearby future.
Its often a task itself to organize our own tasks.
With so many tasks to keep in our mind sometimes we find difficult to keep track of them all if we don’t have a system to keep and manage them.
The SpeedyManager is a mobile application that offers its users a list-based task management system. The application offers its users the capability of creating tasks and lists of tasks and share them with tour friends and partners.

This application can be used by its users in many different contexts such as project management or bug tracking, item shop listing, personal agenda, chatting even image sharing.

Arquitecture and Design

The Speedy Manager is a cloud-based application written in Kotlin and uses the Google Firebase system to store its data in the real-time database. This Google Cloud Service is what makes the back end of this mobile application. With functionalities such as the Firebase Realtime-database, Storage and Authentication this service by Google offers many resources and safety to Android application development.

To justice to its name the application was built for fast operation by the user.
Which in terms of design, means, that the application and its functionalities are quite simple and effective to operate making it very user-friendly.

Tecnologies used

Android

This application only available for Android devices and because it was made for academic purposes is not available on the play store, but you can get it here ;p

Kotlin
